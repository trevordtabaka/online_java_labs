Answer the following questions:

1.) What is inheritance?
Grouping characteristics in a hierarchically classified way
2.) Why do we use inheritance?
You can use it to group common traits in a superclass and let other classes inherit the superclass functionality.

6.) What is the "super" keyword used for?

7.) When a subclass object is created, in what order do the constructors execute?

8.) What is method overwriting?
when a subclass creates a method with the same name as one of its superclasses
9.) How can method overwriting be beneficial?
To make a method more specific to the subclass
